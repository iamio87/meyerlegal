LEGAL SERVICES YOU CAN TRUST

Adrian L. Meyer, Esq. has been providing Doylestown and the surrounding greater Bucks and Montgomery Counties of PA with estate planning, real estate, business, family and other legal services for over 35 years, with a special focus in the Estates and Probate field.

Thoroughly and actively involved in all levels of service to both the professional and civil communities, Mr. Meyer's expertise and genuine concern are expressed by assisting individuals, families and businesses with their various legal needs with a personal touch.

We welcome you to contact us any time to set up an appointment, answer your questions, and assist you in the best way possible.

Services

The Law Offices of Adrian L. Meyer provide many services to the residents of Doylestown and the greater Bucks and Montgomery Counties, concentrating on the Estate field but also in other legal areas. Following are our areas of expertise, but feel free to contact us with questions about any services.

Estate/Elder Law Practice

In this increasingly complex and uncertain world, there is something to be said for careful planning. The long-term illness or death of a loved one can initiate financial and legal issues, when the focus is better placed on the emotional well-being of those affected. Understanding the legal process in advance, ensuring loved ones have the opportunity to make their wishes known and carried out, enabling a family to act as advocate for a silenced patient, can all be achieved with advanced estate planning. We can help you with the following estate tools:

  1. Last Will & Testament
  2. Living Will
  3. Power of Attorney (both Financial and Medical)
  4. Estate and Trust Planning and Administration
  5. Probate
  6. Guardianships

Real Estate Practice

Whether it be commercial or residential, a primary residence, vacation home, condo or co-op, whether you own, buy, sell, lease, rent or rent out, real estate law affects nearly everyone. Protecting yourself and your assets by carefully reviewing any real estate agreements you prepare or are asked to sign is essential; Adrian L. Meyer can help navigate the language and prepare the documents that will suit your needs. Following are some of the services we can provide:

  1. Preparation of Commercial and Residential Real Estate Agreements (Purchase, Sale, Lease, Rental)
  2. Representation at Real Estate Closing
  3. Preparation of Deeds
  4. Other relating to Landlord/Tenant Agreements, Easements, Land Use and Zoning, and more

Government & Municipal Practice

Opening a new business out of your residence? Looking to add a porch to your home? Making a large financial investment to your business or home can become devastating if municipal authorities deem the work unzoned or otherwise illegal to have performed. Work with Adrian L. Meyer to understand and work with the ordinances, regulations, bylaws and decisions that govern a municipality. Here are some of the areas we handle regarding municipal law:

  1. Zoning, including Hearings
  2. Variances
  3. Permits
  4. Special Exceptions
  5. Litigation

Business Law Practice

Virtually every business transaction involves legal documents, written or implied. For small businesses in Bucks and Montgomery Counties, it is essential to have legal counsel available to review important business contracts and agreements. From starting a business to passing it on to the next generation, we can assist with the following items:

  1. Business Formation (all entities: LLC, LLP, Corporations, Partnerships, etc.)
  2. Business Contract Draft and Review
  3. Buy-Sell Agreements
  4. Succession Plans
  5. Corporate Governance

Other Practices

With decades of experience, Adrian L. Meyer focuses on the practices listed above, but also retains clients in other legal practices, or is able to refer them to an appropriate firm for their needs. You are welcome to contact us with questions about the following:

  1. Family Law, including Pre- and Post-Nuptial Agreements, Divorce, Custody Agreements
  2. Personal Injury, including Auto Accidents and Slip & Fall
